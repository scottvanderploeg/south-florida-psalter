Why the Psalms Matter

The Church Has Forgotten Its Songbook

For centuries, the Psalms were the songbook of the church. They shaped worship, formed faith, and gave voice to every human emotion before God. Yet today, most Christians have never sung a single psalm.

The Psalms teach us how to pray, how to lament, how to rejoice, and how to see Christ in all of Scripture. They are God's own words— given to us to sing back to Him.
