About the Project

The South Florida Psalter is a comprehensive musical setting of all 150 Psalms, using the word-for-word text of the NIV 2011 translation. Each psalm has been set to original music, creating a complete songbook for the church to sing God's own words back to Him.

Why the Psalms?

For centuries, the Psalms were the songbook of the church. They shaped worship, formed faith, and gave voice to every human emotion before God. Yet today, most Christians have never sung a single psalm.

The church has forgotten its own songbook — and in doing so, has lost something irreplaceable. The Psalms teach us how to pray, how to lament, how to rejoice, and how to see Christ in all of Scripture.
